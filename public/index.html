The information paradox

We live in the most nutrition-informed era in history — and yet only 3–10% of people have genuine, accurate knowledge of how macronutrients work. The rest navigate a sea of myths: "carbs after 6 pm turn to fat," "fat makes you fat," "you need to detox." Most crash diets are built on these exact misconceptions.

The yo-yo trap

Up to 30% of dieters turn to extreme restriction — 600–800 kcal days, juice cleanses, skipped meals. Short term the scale moves. Long term: muscle loss, a slower metabolism, spiked cortisol, and the weight returns heavier than before. The cycle repeats every year.

The fix is simpler than you think

Decades of research converge on one finding: awareness of what you actually eat is the single most effective behaviour change for sustainable weight management. You don't need a strict plan — you need honest, effortless tracking. That's exactly what Eat Right is built for.

Privacy first

Your data never leaves your phone

Eat Right stores everything in your browser's IndexedDB — no accounts, no servers, no subscriptions. The only external call is to Google Gemini to parse meals (you bring your own free API key).
